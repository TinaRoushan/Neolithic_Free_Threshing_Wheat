Stage 1

CharredNeo_Ref= All charred modern reference material used for Stage 1
CharredNeo_Ref_LR= Subset of CharredNeo_Ref with all advanced/improved varieties removed
Combined_Neo= All Stage 1 modern and archaeological material 
Combined_Neo_LR= Stage 1 landrace/traditional accessions and all archaeological grains
Combined_Neo_LR_Catal= Stage 1 landrace/traditional accessions and Çatalhöyük grains
Combined_Neo_LR_Kouph= Stage 1 landrace/traditional accessions and Kouphovouno grains
Unclass_Catal= Çatalhöyük grains ready for reclassification 
Unclass_Kouph= Kouphovouno grains ready for reclassification
